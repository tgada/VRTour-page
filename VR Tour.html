Instructions for using the VR Tour on this page: You can click and drag on any part of the image to move the image, so you can get a better view. You can click on the information icon (seen to the right) to get more information about this project and instructions. You can click on the images to get views of the interior and exterior of the chapel, as well as some information about those views and the history of the chapel itself. There are two of these images on the first page and five of them on the second page. You can navigate to the second page by clicking on the ">" icon on the bottom of the screen next to the text depicting what scene you are currently observing.
